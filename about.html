ABOUT ME!

HI THERE, I'M BELLA

(OBOOOT)

Hi there, I'm Bella - a graphic designer who loves to combine my passion for technology with art and culture. When I'm not crafting designs, I can be found wandering aimlessly around the city with my earphones on, belting out my favorite tunes.

IF YOU ARE LOOKING FOR

ALL-WORK-NO-PLAY, TYPE OF DESIGNER, YOU'RE IN THE WRONG PLACE

I like to keep things light and fun, and my sense of humor tends to sneak into thing that I do. But don't worry I take my work seriously and am committed to creating designs that not only look good but also communicate effectively.

I hope through this blog, I can share my experiences and insights with other designers, art lovers, and curious minds alike. So come along for the ride as we explore the exciting world of design and all that it has to offer. And who knows, maybe I'll even manage to sneak in a joke or two along the way.
